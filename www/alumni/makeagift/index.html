Giving to the Department Through Your Estate

Supporting the School of Physics and Astronomy in your estate plan is a simple way to make a profound impact. Although there are several ways to make such a gift, one of the easiest is placing a bequest to the School in your will. This has the advantage of not depleting needed income for living expenses, and often allows you to make a more financially significant gift than would have been possible during life.

Contact Physics Development Officer, Shannon Weiher, for all questions and inquiries regarding private giving.
