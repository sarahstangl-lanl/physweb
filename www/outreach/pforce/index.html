About

The Physics Force is a K-12 outreach program through the University of Minnesota. Our program's greatest success is generating an interest in science in K-12 students. We go above and beyond to educate and entertain. Physics Force does things on a grand scale to bring excitement and wonder to the thousands of students who attend our shows every year.

  • Do you want the Physics Force to perform for your school or group? Go to our contact page

  • Would you like to learn more about the demonstrations Physics Force does? Check out our demonstrations page.

Physics Force is supported by the School of Physics and Astronomy, the Dean of the College of Science and Engineering, and the University of Minnesota's Materials Research Science and Engineering Center (MRSEC).
