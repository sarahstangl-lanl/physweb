For students, new faculty and long-term visitors

You can choose freely between living on campus or off. The University has a housing bureau to aid students and faculty in finding affordable housing. For those living off campus, the Twin Cities have a reliable and extensive mass transit system with numerous bus lines connecting outlying areas to various points on campus. The University itself also operates numerous express and shuttle buses from many locations in the cities, directly to and from campus, and between many campus locations. <%method related>
